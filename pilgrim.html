Taman Makam Pahlawan Kalibata

The National Main Heroes Cemetery in Kalibata, colloquially known as Kalibata Heroes Cemetery, is a military cemetery in Kalibata, South Jakarta, Indonesia. It was built in 1953 and opened on 10 November 1954.

Margarana

Taman Pujaan Bangsa Margarana National Monument is a memorial monument erected to commemorate the Puputan Margarana tragedy, in Marga Village, Marga District, Tabanan Regency, Bali. On November 20, 1946, there was an all-out battle between the troops of the Republic of Indonesia against the Dutch colonialists, at Banjar Kelaci, Marga Village, by Ciung Wanara troops under the command of Colonel I Gusti Ngurah Rai.
