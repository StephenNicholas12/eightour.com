Jakarta Fair

Jakarta Fair 2023 or the Jakarta Raya Fair (PRJ) has officially opened and will be held for 33 days. This event will start from 14 June to 16 July 2023 at JIExpo Kemayoran. One of the events most awaited by visitors is the Jakarta Fair music concert. This year the Jakarta Fair music concert will be held routinely for 32 days except for the Eid al-Adha takbiran night (June 28). One of the uniqueness of the Jakarta Fair music concert is that it presents cross-genre music. Starting from Rock, Pop, Jazz, Balad, Dangdut, Koplo, Reggae, Alternative, to punk music.
