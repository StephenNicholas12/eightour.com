Ogoh-Ogoh Parade

A number of youths paraded the Ogoh-Ogoh during a series of 2023 Ogoh-Ogoh Festival activities at the Garuda Wisnu Kencana (GWK) Cultural Park, Badung, Bali, Sunday (26/3/2023). The ogoh-ogoh performance which involves hundreds of artists is staged to appreciate and preserve tradition as well as to introduce the richness of Balinese art and culture to tourists visiting GWK.
