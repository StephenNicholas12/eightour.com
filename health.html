Toya Devasya

Toya Devasya Hot Spring Waterpark boasts seven different hot spring pools for you to dip in and 2 non hot spring pools. The water is heated from inside the earth, so you will soak in a lot of natural minerals. Enjoy infinity pool overlooking the lake at Toya Devasya Hot spring is so useful to heal your mind, body, and soul

Angseri Hot Spring

This hot spring is located in Angseri Village, Baturiti District, Tabanan Regency. You need about 55 minutes driving from downtown Tabanan to this place. Angseri Hot Spring has a good design, surrounded by beautiful gardens, and located at the bottom of a riverbank valley. You can feel the cool air, combined with the warmth of natural water.
