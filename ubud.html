Ubud Food Festival

UFF has been considered the most exciting cross-cultural culinary gathering in the region. The festival puts in the spotlight Indonesia’s diverse cuisine, emerging and established chefs, as well as extraordinary local produce—all in a celebration that takes place in the beautiful landscape of Ubud, Bali’s cultural centre. The theme of this year’s UFF is ‘soil’.

This theme is a tribute to the living element that provides 95% of the food we eat. The festival will explore this nourishing and natural resource that makes life on Earth possible. Keeping in theme with this, UFF 2023’s entire program will feature a fine balance between feasting on Indonesia’s diverse cuisine, whilst exploring the most recent gastronomic trends in regard to environmental conservation and sustainability.
