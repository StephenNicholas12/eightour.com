The Merdeka Building

The Merdeka Building on Jalan Asia-Afrika, Bandung, Indonesia, is a historic building that was used as the venue for the 1955 Asian-African Summit. Now this building is used as a museum displaying various collectibles and photos of the Asian-African Conference which was the forerunner of the first Non-Aligned Movement ever held here in 1955.
